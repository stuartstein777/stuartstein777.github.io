Peanut Butter Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • 4 tbspn peanut butter
  • 2 tbspn maple syrup
  • 4 tbspn soy sauce
  • garlic finely chopped
  • ginger finely chopped
  • green chilli finely chopped
  • Chilli sauce (to taste)
  • sesame oil
  • Sesama Seeds
  • chilli flakes
  • 1 tbspn rice wine vinegar
  • groundnut oil

Method

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Mix the peanut butter, maple syrup, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, chilli sauce and sesame oil to a bowl and mix
  3. Sautee the garlic, ginger, chilli, chilli flakes and sesame seeds in a little groundnut oil
  4. Drain the noodles once cooked and add to the garlic, ginger, chilli mix, mix well.
  5. Add some noodle water if the sauce needs thickened
