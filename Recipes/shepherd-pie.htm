Shepherds Pie

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Lamb Mince
  • Red Wine
  • Potatos
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Chicken stock
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcester Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Olive Oil

Method

  1. Browne the mince
  2. Add the mushrooms, carrots, onion, rosemary, thyme, and garlic. Cook till softened
  3. Add the tomato paste, cook for a few mins
  4. Add the worcester sauce, cook for a couple of mins
  5. Add the red wine, cook till the red wine is reduced
  6. Add the chicken stock, bring to a simmer. Cook for 30 mins
  7. Peel and boil the potatos
  8. Top a garlic bulb, place in foil and drizzle with oil. Put in the oven and cook until the cloves are soft
  9. Mash the potatos, mix in the softened roasted garlic with lots of butter
  10. Mix in an egg yolk
  11. Combine the mashed potato on top of the mince mixture
  12. Grate parmesan cheese over the forked mash
  13. Finish in oven for 30-40 mins
