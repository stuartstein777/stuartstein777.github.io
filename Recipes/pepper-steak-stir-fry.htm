Pepper Steak Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • Red Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Spring Onion
  • Radishes
  • Pak Choi
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Black Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Baking Soda
  • Oil

Method

  1. Cut steak into thin pieces
  2. Sprinkle over cornstarch
  3. Oyster Sauce
  4. Pinch of baking soda
  5. Some oil
  6. Mix together, set aside.
  7. Fry the vegetables in wok for a few minutes
  8. Add the steak, fry for a minutes
  9. Add the sauce, fry for a few minutes
  10. Serve with noodles.
