Breakfast Burrito with Crispy potato sides

Ingredients

  • 2x tortilla wraps
  • 2x eggs
  • 2x sausages
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Smokey German Cheese
  • Mayo 2tbspns
  • Baby Potatos
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • Paprika (or cajun seasoning)
  • Hot Sauce (1tspn or so to taste)
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Oil

Method

  1. Cut/Grate the cheese into small pieces
  2. Cube the potatos into 1.5cm cubes
  3. Heat oil in pan
  4. Add the potato cubes and cook for 15-18 mins
  5. In the same pan, cook the sausages
  6. When the potatos have 5 minutes to go sautee the onion and bell pepper
  7. Scramble the eggs with the sausage and onion / pepper mixture
  8. Add the cheese to melt
  9. Warm through the tortillas
  10. For the sauce mix lime juice, mayo, paprika, hot sauce
  11. Add sausages, onions, pepper and egg to the tortillas with the sauce and roll
  12. Serve with the crispy tender potato cubs
