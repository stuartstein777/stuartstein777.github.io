Coconut Cod Curry

Ingredients

  • Cod Loin
  • Bell Pepper
  • Spring Onion
  • Garlic
  • chilli powder (½ tspn)
  • Curry Powder (1 tspn)
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • Lime Juice
  • Rice

Method

  1. Sear the cod: Lightly season cod with salt. Sear in a hot oiled pan for 2 mins per side until lightly golden (don’t cook through). Set aside.
  2. Cook aromatics: In the same pan, sauté garlic, spring onion, and sliced bell pepper.
  3. Add chilli powder and any other warm spices you like (cumin, paprika, turmeric…).
  4. Add coconut milk: Stir in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add cod back: Gently nestle the cod back into the sauce. Cover and simmer on low for 6–8 minutes until cooked through.
  6. Finish with lime: Squeeze over lime juice to brighten.
  7. Serve over steamed basmati rice.
