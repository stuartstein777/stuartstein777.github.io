Tomato and Peanut Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Onions
  • Sweet Potato
  • Ginger paste
  • Garlic Paste
  • Tinned Tomatos
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chicken Stock
  • Dried Corriander
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne
  • Cumin

Method

  1. Fry the chicken thighs to brown, remove from pan
  2. Fry off onions and sweet potato (3 to 4 mins)
  3. Add garlic paste and ginger paste (tablespoon)
  4. Add dried coriander, paprika, cayenne and cumin
  5. Add tinned tomatos
  6. Add chicken stock
  7. Add peanut butter
  8. Serve with rice
