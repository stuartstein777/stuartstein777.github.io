Spicey Beef Arayes

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Pita breads
  • Parsley or Basil
  • Cayenne
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Green chilli
  • Chilli oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Method

  1. Blend up parsley (or basil), onion, garlic, green chilli.
  2. Wrap in cheese cloth and squeeze out all moisture
  3. Add in the cayenne, salt, pepper, chillli flakes and smoked paprika
  4. Mix with the mince
  5. Stuff the mince mixture into pita breads. Not too thick!
  6. Brush the outside of the pitas with chilli oil
  7. Fry the pitas in a griddle pan until the mince is cooked through
