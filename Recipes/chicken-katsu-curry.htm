Chicken Katsu Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Sunflower Oil / Groundnut oil
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil / groundnut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 thumb sized piece of ginger, very finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground tumeric
  • 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Method

  1. First make the sauce
  2. Heat the oil in a pan
  3. Add the onion, fry for 5-6 mins until soft
  4. Add the garlic and ginger, fry for 1-2 mins
  5. Add the curry powder, turmeric, chilli powder, tomato puree, stir well
  6. Pour in the chicken stock, bring to a steady simmer
  7. Cook for 8 mins
  8. Add the rice wine vinger, honey and soy sauce, cook for a few mins
  9. Blitz the sauce together with a hand blender
  10. For the chicken
  11. Flatten the chicken to a uniform thickness, 1cm thick or so
  12. Dredge in flour, egg, then panko breadcrumbs
  13. Fry in a pan until golden and the chicken is cooked, few mins a side
  14. Serve with rice and the sauce
