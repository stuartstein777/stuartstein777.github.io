Honey Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Groundnut oil
  • Cornflour
  • Soy Sauce
  • White pepper
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Honey
  • Sesame oil

Method

  1. Dice the chicken breast(s)
  2. Marinade in soy sauce, egg, white pepper
  3. Cover in some cornflour
  4. Add a lot of oil to a wok
  5. Fry the chicken till crispy on the outside
  6. Remove from the pan, drain most of the oil
  7. Add some butter to the pan
  8. Then add some soy sauce, sesame oil, chilli flakes and honey
  9. Add more butter, bring to a froth
  10. Add back in the chicken
  11. Toss well till the chicken is all coated
  12. Serve with Rice and spring onions
