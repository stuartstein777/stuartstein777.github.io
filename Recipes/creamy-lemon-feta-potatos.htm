Creamy Lemon Feta Potatos

Ingredients

  • Baby Potatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Rocket
  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Boil the baby potatoes in salted water
  2. Drain and cut each potato in half
  3. Add back into pan on the heat with some butter
  4. Add in the lemon juice, and feta cheese and rocket
  5. Cook until the rocket is wilted
  6. You want the sauce to be creamy, add lemon juice / butter as needed
  7. Season and serve
