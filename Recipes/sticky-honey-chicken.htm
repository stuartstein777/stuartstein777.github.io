Sticky Honey Chicken with Rocket and Parmesan Salad

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Runny Honey
  • Tomato Puree
  • Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Lime
  • Olive Oil
  • Crusty Bread
  • Salt
  • Rocket
  • 3tbspns Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4tbspns Balsamic Oil
  • Crusty bread
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pine Nuts (can be toasted)

Method

  1. Pre-heat a griddle pan to very hot.
  2. Put the chicken breasts on a chopping board between two sheets of clingfilm. Use a meat hammer to flatten the chicken into 1cm thick
  3. Put the honey, tomato puree, smoked paprika and 2 pinches of salt in a bowl. Pour the lime juice and olive oil, mix together. Add the chicken and coat in the marinade.
  4. Cook the chicken on the griddle pan, 6 minutes a side.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare the salad by placing rocket leaves and pine nuts in a large bow. Pour over olive oil and balsamic vinegar and season with a couple of pinches of salt. Toss. Scatter shaved parmesan on top.
  6. Serve with some crusty bread.
