Tray Bake Cajun Salmon and Baby Potatos

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Baby potatos
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Mayonnaise (1 tbspns)
  • Lemon juice (half in the sauce, half over the dish)
  • Yoghurt (3 tbspns)
  • Dijon Mustard (1 tspn)
  • Honey (1 tspn)
  • Paprika (pinch)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Oil

Method

  1. Slice the baby potatoes and seasoning in oil and cajun seasoning, salt and pepper. Add to the baking tray and bake for 15 minutes
  2. Chop the onions and pepper into large sections, add to the same tray and bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Coat the salmon in oil and cajun seasoning. Add to the tray and cook for 8-12 minutes until it's perfect.
  4. For the dipping sauce mix the sauce ingredients with some salt and pepper
  5. Squeeze lime juice over to serve
