Spicey Indonesian Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Groundnut oil
  • 1 tsp spicy belacan paste
  • 1-2 tbsp ketjap manis
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • Egg
  • Additional veg (carrot, pepper, spring onion)
  • Juice of ½ Lime

Method

  1. Boil the rice. Leave to cool
  2. Heat oil in a large wok
  3. Add onion and garlic and belacan paste, fry 1-2 mins until fragrant
  4. Add any veg, cook until just soft
  5. Add in the rice, stir to break up clumps
  6. Drizzle in the ketjap manis, stir to coat all the rice
  7. Add chilli flakes
  8. Squeeze over lime juice
  9. Serve with fried egg on top (with runny yolk)
