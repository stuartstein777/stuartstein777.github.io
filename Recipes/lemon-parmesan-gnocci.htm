Lemon Parmesan Gnocci

Ingredients

  • Gnocci
  • Butter
  • Grated Parmesan (very finely grated)
  • Black Pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Lemon zest

Method

  1. Boil the gnocci
  2. When its done, drain and retain some of the pasta water
  3. Add pasta back to the pot
  4. Add in the lemon juice and butter, cook to melt the butter
  5. Add in the parmesan and the reserved pasta water. Cook until it thickens and is creamy
