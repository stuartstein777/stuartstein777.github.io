Chicken with Tomatos, mozzarella and Basil

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 400g can of cherry tomatos
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Sliced Mozzarrela balls (not buffalo mozzarella)
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Slash each chicken breast four times with a sharp knife, cutting as far as the middle of the breast
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat and gently fry the garlic and the chicken for 2 minutes on each side until golden all over
  3. Tip in the cherry tomatos with the basil and season with salt and pepper
  4. Cook uncovered, over a medium heat for 12 minutes. Halfway through cooking, turn over the chicken breast
  5. Meanwhile preheat the grill to highest setting
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and place the mozzarella slices on top of the chicken breast. Grind over some black pepper and put the pan under the the grill for about 1 minute
  7. Serve with garlic bread
