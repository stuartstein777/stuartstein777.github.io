Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • 2x 400g tinned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Beef Stock
  • Celery
  • Pancetta
  • Rosemary
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato puree
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan
  • Basil

Method

  1. Cook the garlic and vegetables till soft with the pancetta
  2. Add the rosemary
  3. Add the beef and brown and seal
  4. Add the red wine, cookoff
  5. Add the tomatoes and tomato puree
  6. Simmer for an hour
  7. Before serving, add serving of the bolognese to a pan, add some pasta water, then add the pasta.
  8. Finish with some parmessan and basil
