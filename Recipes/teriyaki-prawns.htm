Teriyaki Prawns

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4cm piece of ginger
  • raw peeled jumbo pranws
  • nest of medium egg noodles
  • edamame beans
  • tablespoon chilli oil
  • tablespoon sesame seeds
  • teriyaki sauce

Method

  1. Peel and finely slice the garlic
  2. Peel and matchstick the ginger
  3. De-vein and Butterfly the prawns
  4. Get a pan on a high heat
  5. Place the noodles and edamame beans in the pan, cook for 4 minutes and drain. Reserve some of the water
  6. Put the pan back on medium heat
  7. Add the chilli oil, garlic and ginger
  8. Cook until lightly golden
  9. Toss in the prawns and sesame seeds until prawns are just cooked, then add the teriyaki sauce
  10. Toss in the noodles and edamame beans, add back in some of noodle water if needed
  11. Serve with some extra chilli oil if wanted
