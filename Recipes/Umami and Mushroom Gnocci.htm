Umami and Mushroom Gnocci

Ingredients

  • Gnocci
  • Mushrooms
  • Lemon zest
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Parmesan
  • Umami paste

Method

  1. Grate the parmesan.
  2. Boil the gnocci till it floats.
  3. Fry the mushrooms in some butter and minced garlic till they are deep brown.
  4. Move mushrooms to one side of the pan
  5. Add the cooked gnocci with some more butter. Don't stir, leave to crisp a bit.
  6. Stir in a 1 tsp of umami paste with some reserved water from the gnocci
  7. Mix till it emulsifies
  8. Mix the mushrooms in with it all
  9. Add the grated parmesan, let it melt. Add more water and touch more umami paste if needed.
  10. Finish with some lemon zest.
