Chilli Jam and Feta Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Chilli Jam
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Red Onion

Method

  1. Place the salmon flesh side down
  2. Cut through the flesh of the salmon at 1cm intervals
  4. Spread chilli jam over the salmon
  5. Add some oil to a pan, heat
  6. Add the salmon skin side down for 2-3 minutes till the skin crisps up
  7. Transfer to an oven for 5 minutes
  8. Serve with the feta cheese crumbled over and sliced red onion
  9. Good with baby potatoes
