Bangers and Mash

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Sausages
  • Mustard
  • Worcester sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cornflour
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Beef stock

Method

  1. Boil potatoes until knife soft
  2. Meanwhile, roast a whole garlic bulb, with the top cut off and drizzled with olive oil
  3. Mix the garlic with the potatoes and butter, and mash
  4. Thinly slice the onions
  5. Add butter to a pan and gently fry the onions
  6. This should take about 40-50 mins, add more butter if it gets too dry
  7. Add beef stock, mustard and worcester sauce
  8. Finish by adding a cornflour water slurry to thicken if needed
  9. Fry or Grill the sausages
  10. Good with garlic bread
