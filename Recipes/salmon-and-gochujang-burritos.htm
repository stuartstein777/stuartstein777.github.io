Salmon and Gochujang Burritos

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Light Mayonnaise
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Spring Onion
  • Carrot
  • Hoison Sauce
  • Gochujang Paste
  • Tortillas

Method

  1. Mix some hoison saue and gochujang paste in a bowl
  2. Mix the chopped spring onions, garlic, carrot and mayonnaise in a bowl
  3. Fry the salmon skin down in a warm oven proof pan for a couple of minutes
  4. Transfer the pan to the oven at 180 for 5 minutes
  5. Spread some of the mayo and veg mix on a warmed tortilla. Add the salmon and spread over the hoison and gochujang mix
  6. Wrap the tortillas
