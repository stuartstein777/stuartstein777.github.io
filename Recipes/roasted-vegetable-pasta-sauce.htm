Roasted Vegetable Pasta Sauce

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Red Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Bell Peppers
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Method

  1. Roast the vegetables, can wrap garlic in foil, drizzled all with oil
  2. After roasted, add to blender
  3. Add the basil, tomato sauce, salt and pepper to blender
  4. Blend all together
  5. Add to cooked pastas
