Yellow Rice and Chicken

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin
  • Buter
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Chicken
  • Lemon Juice (1.5 lemons)
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Oregano (3 tbspns)
  • Ground Coriander (1 tsp)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Red Chilli Powder (1 tsp)
  • Cumin (½ tsp)
  • Garam Masala (½ tsp)
  • Salt
  • Tumeric (½ tsp)
  • Light Mayo (4 tbspns)
  • Greek Yoghurt (8 tbspns)
  • Vinegar (¾ tsp)
  • Black Papper (¾ tsp)
  • Sugar
  • Water

Method

  1. Prepar the chickem marinade
  2. Mix the garlic cloves, lemon juice, greek yoghurt, ground coriander, oregano, paprika, red chilli powder, cumin, garam masala, tumeric and salt in a bowl
  3. Dice the chicken and marinate
  4. Prepare the white sauce
  5. Mix the light may, 2-3 tsps of lemon juice, Greek Yoghurt, vinegar, salt and pepper and sugar. Add water if needed to loosen. Set aside
  6. Melt some butter in a sauce pan
  7. Add some tumeric and cumin
  8. Toast for a few mins
  9. Add in the rice, toast with the spices for a few mins
  10. Add water and cook the rice
  11. Fry the marinaded chicken
  12. Serve the chicken over the rice, drizzle over the white sauce
