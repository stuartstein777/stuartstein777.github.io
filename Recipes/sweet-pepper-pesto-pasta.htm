Sweet Pepper Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • 125g fresh lasagne sheets
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 10g smoked almonds
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ x 460g jar of roasted peppers
  • parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Cut the lasange sheets in half lengthways to make lasagnetti
  2. Peel and finely grate the garlic
  3. Place on a chopping board with the almonds, oregano and peppers
  4. Finely grate the parmesan over the top
  5. Add boiling salted water to a pan, and add the pasta
  6. Boil for a few minutes to cook the pasta
  7. Meanwhile finely chop up everything together on the board to make a rustic pesto
  8. Drain pasta, and add all the ingredients from the board (reserve some of the pasta water and add as needed till its nice and saucy
  9. Finish with some extra virgin oil
