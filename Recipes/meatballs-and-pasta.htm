Meatballs and Pasta in Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • Tagliatelli
  • Minced beef
  • Dried chilli flakes
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pinch of ground cinammon
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Egg
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Fresh basil
  • Red Chilli
  • 2 (400g) tin of tinned tomatos
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Make the meatballs
  2. Mix in a bowl - the mince, chilli flakes, some grated parmesan, an egg, cinammon, crushed garlic
  3. Portion into meatballs
  4. Place in fridge while you make the sauce
  5. For the sauce:
  6. Pierce the chilli with the tip of a knife and cut down lengthways
  7. Add some olive oil to a large pan
  8. Gently fry some garlic and the red chilli and basil stalks
  9. Add in the tinned tomatos
  10. Add in a glug of red wine vinegar
  11. Gently simmer for 30 mins
  12. Cook the pasta
  13. Gently fry the meatballs in a little oil till they are cooked through, turning regularly
