Cajun Hot Dog

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog
  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Pineapple
  • Mayo
  • Hot Sauce

Method

  1. Chop the pepper, onion and pineapple, and mix it all with cajun seasoning
  2. Fry the above mix
  3. Mix the mayo and hot sauce
  4. Cook the dog and warm the bun
  5. Add the pepper, onion and pineapple mix to the bun with the dog
  6. Finish with the spicy mayo mix
