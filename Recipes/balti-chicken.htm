Balti Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • 3 tbspn ghee or vegetable oil
  • 3 tomatos, sliced
  • large onion, sliced
  • ½ tsp kalonji seeds
  • 4 black peppercorns
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garlic puree
  • 1 tsp ginger puree
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp plain yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp chopped, fresh, corriander
  • 2 fresh green chillis, deseed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp lime juice

Method

  1. Dice the chicken breast(s)
  2. Heat the ghee or oil in a large pan
  3. Add the onions and cook for 10 mins on a low heat, stirring occasinally
  4. Add the sliced tomatos, kalonji seeds, peppercorns, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, garam masala, garlic puree, ginger puree and chilli powder, season with salt
  5. Cook for 5 mins stirring constantly
  6. Add the chicken and cook, stirring constantly for 5 minutes. All chicken should be coated
  7. Stir in the yoghurt. It should be room temp. Add partially and quickly stir it in, dont want it to split.
  8. Cook for 10 mins
  9. Stir in the coriander, lime juice and chillis
  10. Serve with naan breads and rice
