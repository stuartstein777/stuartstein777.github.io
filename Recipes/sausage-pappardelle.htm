Sausage Pappardelle

Ingredients

  • 125g fresh lasagne sheets
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • bunch of flat leaf parsley
  • 1 pork sausage
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • red wine
  • 200ml passata
  • parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Cut the lasagne sheets lengthways into 3cm strips to make pappardelle
  2. Peel and finely slice the garlic
  3. Finely chop the top leafy half of the parsley, then the stalks. Keep them seperate
  4. Put a large frying pan on a large heat
  5. Add a little olive oil
  6. Squeeze the sausage meet out of the skin into the pan. Break it up
  7. Fry and stir for 2 minutes
  8. Add the garlic, parsley stalks and fennel seeds
  9. Once slightly golden add a splash of red wine, let it cook away
  10. Add the passata
  11. Add the pasta
  12. Add enough boiling water to just cover the pasta
  13. Let it bubble away for 4 minutes, or until the pasta has absorbed most of the water. Stir regularly
  14. Stir in the parsley leaf
  15. Finish with grated parmesan and some extra virgin olive oil
