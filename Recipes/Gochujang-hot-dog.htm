Gochujang Hot Dog

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog
  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Gochujang Paste
  • Mayo
  • Honey
  • Grated carrot
  • Egg
  • Sesame seeds

Method

  1. Mix the gochujang paste, the mayo and the honey
  2. Toast the sesame seeds
  3. Fry the egg
  4. Make slits in the hot dog and fry
  5. Heat the bun in the pan to finish
  6. Add the mayo paste mix to the bun. Add the carrot, add the dog, then some more paste over the hotdog and sprinkle over the sesame seeds
  7. Finish with the fried egg over the top.
