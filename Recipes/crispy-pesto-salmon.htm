Crispy Pesto Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Asparagus
  • Green pesto
  • Lemon
  • dried chilli flakes
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • rocket
  • Baby potatoes
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Peel and finely slice the garlic
  2. Snap the woody ends off the asparagus. Chop into 2cm chunks
  3. Dry fry the asparagus, tossing regulary
  4. Place the salmon skin side down on greasproof paper
  5. Cut through the flesh of the salmon at 1cm intervals
  6. Rub over the pesto, getting it into the gaps that were sliced
  7. finely grate over the lemon zest
  8. sprinkle over chilli flakes
  9. Drizzle with a little olive oil
  10. fold over the greaseproof paper and press down on the salmon
  11. Add salmon the pan with asparagus, crumb side up
  12. Finish the salmon under the grill
  13. Drain cooked baby potatoes
  14. Add back to a warm pan with the rocket and asparagus, and a little oil till the rocket is wilted. Squeeze in some lemon juice
