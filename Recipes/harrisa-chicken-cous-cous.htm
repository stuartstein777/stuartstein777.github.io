Harrisa Chicken and Cous Cous

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Chilli
  • Harrisa Paste
  • Chicken breast
  • Cous cous

Method

  1. Mix 2tblspns Greek yoghurt & 2tspns of harrisa paste, coat diced chicken, marinade
  2. Fry onions, tomatos, carrots, pepper, garlic and chilli, with teaspoon of harrisa paste
  3. Fry the chicken in the griddle pan
  4. Serve with cous cous and garlic bread
