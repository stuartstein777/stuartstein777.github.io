Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Peppers
  • Green Chillis
  • Garlic
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Tinned Tomatos
  • Tinned Kidney Beans (drained)
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Ground Cumin
  • Tomato Puree
  • Onion
  • Coriander
  • Powdered smoke

Method

  1. Brown mince
  2. Remove mince from pan
  3. Add the onions, peppers, chilli and garlic to same pan. sautee
  4. Add the beef back in
  5. Add the oregano, smoked paprika, tomato paste, powdered smoke. Cook for a few mins
  6. Add the tinned tomatos and corriander
  7. Cook for 30-40 mins
  8. Add the kidney beans, cook for 10 mins
  9. When finishing add the dark chocolate
